What is UX Design?

UX design refers to “user experience design,” which encompasses not only digital sectors, but physical as well. UX design solves problems involving user-product interactions. When people think of UX design they might think of web/mobile app design, but general UX design is more broad.


The root of being a UX designer is researching and developing for users to make better experiences.

What is this site?

This is a cluster of UI/UX design resources for beginners. Similar sites exist (please see the Resource of Resources section of this site), but I've highlighted resources that I recommend. Hope you can find it helpful and enjoy!
