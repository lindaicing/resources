Open source illustrations

Diagram Makers

Cacoo - for teams, paid after 14 day free trial. Really good for flowcharts and easily exportable

FlowMapp - Free up to 1 board, 100 MB space, and 50 pages

Miro - Free up to 1 board. Good for customer journey maps

Mural - Free trial for 30 days, then $12/mos

XTensio - Free up to 1 board. Good for making User Personas.

For better organization (with automation)

Notion - Collaborative, has to-do-lists, IOS and android apps available, desktop apps available. Free personal plan using .edu email

Monday - Work Operating System (Work OS) that powers teams to run projects and workflows

Trello - project organization, automation, and management

MockupWorld - Can mock up your designs in open-source pre-made 3D

PlaceIt - needs subscription

UI Store Design - Mockups

Unsplash - Beautiful, free images and photos that you can download and use for any project.

Pexels - Has lots of ads

Coolors - Color palette generator, can tweak and save palettes. Very easy to use
